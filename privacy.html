Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how WebP2JPG (webptojpeg.vercel.app) handles information when you use our free image conversion tool.

Your image files

WebP2JPG does not upload, store, or transmit your image files. All image processing happens entirely within your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API and the W3C FileReader API. When you select a WebP file, it is read by your browser's JavaScript engine locally — no copy is sent to any server. The converted JPG file is also generated locally and downloaded directly to your device. WebP2JPG has no server-side image processing infrastructure.

Information we do not collect

  • Image files or their contents
  • File names or metadata
  • Personal identification information
  • Account or registration data (there are no accounts)
  • Session recordings or heatmaps
  • Keystroke or form input data

Third-party services

WebP2JPG uses two third-party services that may collect limited data:

  • Google Fonts — loads typography from Google's CDN. Google may log font requests. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense — displays advertising to support the free service. AdSense may use cookies to show relevant ads. See Google's advertising policies. You can opt out via Google Ad Settings.

The page is hosted on Vercel, which may log standard web server data (IP address, browser type, page URL, timestamp) for infrastructure and security purposes. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.

Cookies

WebP2JPG itself does not set any cookies. Google AdSense and Google Fonts may set cookies through their respective services as described above.

Children's privacy

WebP2JPG does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of WebP2JPG after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us via GitHub (oscargml) or visit our About page.
