How It Works

This tool uses Client-Side Processing. Unlike other converters, we don't upload your files to a server. Your browser does all the heavy lifting using the HTML5 Canvas API, ensuring your privacy is 100% protected.

Why convert WebP to JPG?

WebP is great for the web, but JPG remains the king of compatibility. Whether you're uploading to an old government portal, a printing service, or a basic photo editor, JPG works everywhere.
